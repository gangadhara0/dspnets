Easy Steps for Pigeon Safety Nets Installation

Step - 01

Assessment of the Area

Measure the installation space, such as balconies, windows, or terraces, to determine the net size and requirements.

Step - 02

Material Selection

Choose high-quality, durable pigeon nets that are weather-resistant and suitable for the specific area.

Step - 04
